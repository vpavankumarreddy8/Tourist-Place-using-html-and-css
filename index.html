Tourist & Cultural Guide

Tourist Places

Arunachalam

Located in Tamil Nadu, this temple is renowned for its massive size, intricate architecture, and spiritual significance.

Kerala

Known as "God's Own Country," Kerala is famous for its stunning backwaters, lush greenery, and rich cultural heritage.

Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple

Located in Nellore, this temple is a famous spiritual landmark in Andhra Pradesh.

Adventure & Activities

MGB Felicity Mall

One of the largest malls in Andhra Pradesh with top national & international brands and food chains.

Srisailam Dam

Constructed across the Krishna River, it is the 2nd largest working hydroelectric station in India.

Swimming

A popular recreational and fitness activity enjoyed in both fresh and saltwater environments.

Sports

Football

The most popular sport in the world, played between two teams of eleven players.

Cricket

A bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players.

Basketball

A fast-paced sport where two teams of five players aim to score points by shooting into the hoop.

Festival Celebration

Diwali

Festival of Lights symbolizing the victory of good over evil, celebrated with lamps, sweets, and fireworks.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Celebrated with devotion to Lord Ganesha for ten days with colorful processions and rituals.

Pongal

A Tamil harvest festival named after the ceremonial dish "Pongal", prepared with rice, milk, and jaggery.
